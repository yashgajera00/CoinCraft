1. Bitcoin
Name: Bitcoin
Invented by: Satoshi Nakamoto (2008 white paper, launched 2009)
Highest Price: ~$124,457 (August 14, 2025)
Lowest Price: ~$0.0000–0.0009 (in early 2009)
History:
- 2009: Genesis block mined; network begins.
- 2010: First real-world purchase — 10,000 BTC for two pizzas.
- 2013: Rose to ~$1,000; later plunged.
- 2017: Peaked near $20,000, followed by correction.
- 2021: Briefly surpassed $60,000 multiple times.
- 2024: Broke the $100,000 mark amid growing ETF demand.
- 2025: Set new ATH at ~$124K in August.
